Current Events

In 2021, the United States Army Special Operations Command (USASOC) conducted a study on women in Army Special Operations Forces (ARSOF).

The study aimed to identify challenges and barriers women face in these units and to promote inclusivity by removing obstacles to their full participation, ultimately enhancing diversity and operational effectiveness.

USASOC News Service

Official news and updates on U.S. military operations are available through the USASOC News Service. Army Special Operations Forces: Psychological Operations, Civil Affairs, Rangers, Green Berets, etc.

In order to keep service members, their families, and the general public informed about Army SOF actions both locally and abroad, it provides news releases, stories, images, and videos that highlight important missions, training, and accomplishments.
